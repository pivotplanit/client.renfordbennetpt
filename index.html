Recommended Strategy: Local Dominance Through Consistency

You can't out-resource Healthy Mummy (139K monthly visitors) or Fit Mother Project (517K YouTube subscribers). But you CAN dominate local UK search and build a loyal community of London-area moms.

Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

  • Instagram Priority: Post 4x/week with real training footage and mom lifestyle content
  • Facebook Secondary: Build community group for UK moms
  • TikTok Testing: Short-form video for discovery
  • GMB Optimization: Local search is your fastest win
  • Content Style: Video/photo-based (NOT graphic-heavy) showing real results

Phase 2: Authority Building (Months 4-12)

  • Blog Monthly: Topics like "Fitting Fitness Around School Runs," "Quick Workouts for Busy Moms"
  • Local Partnerships: Collaborate with UK parenting blogs, local gyms, mom groups
  • Client Success Stories: Heavy emphasis on real testimonials and transformations
  • SEO Focus: Target "personal trainer London moms," "busy mum fitness UK," local variations

Content Differentiation

  • Your Angle: "Real PT, Real Mom, Real Results" - not just online programs
  • Authenticity Over Polish: iPhone videos of actual training sessions beat stock graphics
  • Local Community: Feature London parks, local moms, UK-specific challenges (weather, school terms)
  • Hybrid Model: Your face-to-face + online options beat pure online competitors
