Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the Reset program for?

The Reset programs are perfect for busy mums who want to reclaim their energy and confidence. Whether you've never exercised before or you're getting back into fitness after having children, the program adapts to your current level. It's especially good for women who struggle to find time for themselves and need a structured plan that fits into a hectic family life.

How much time do I need to commit each week?

The Reset programs require just 3-4 workouts per week, each lasting 30-45 minutes. That's less than 3 hours total per week. Many clients break these into smaller sessions when needed - even 15-minute workouts add up! The nutrition component is designed around family-friendly meals, so you won't be cooking separate dinners.

Do I need special equipment or a gym membership?

Not at all! The Reset programs are designed to be done at home with minimal equipment. You'll need a set of dumbbells (or household items as substitutes) and a resistance band, which I can help you source affordably. As you progress, you might choose to add more equipment, but it's never required.

I've tried programs before and failed. How is this different?

Most programs fail because they're not designed for real life - especially the demanding life of a busy mum. My approach focuses on sustainability and flexibility. We have contingency plans for sick kids, work emergencies, and those days when you just can't face a workout. Plus, you get direct access to me for accountability and troubleshooting. The entire program is built around the reality of a busy mum's schedule.
